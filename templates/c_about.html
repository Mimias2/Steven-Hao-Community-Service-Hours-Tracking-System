Welcome to the Community Service Hours Tracking System!


Our mission is to provide a simple, effective way to keep track of all of your Community Service Hours. The Community Service Hours Tracking System allows you to log, edit, and view reports for all of your community service activities. Easily create new hour logs using the NewHours page. You can also access all of your own logs with the MyHours page or view all of your fellow classmates' hours with the SchoolHours page. Reports for your logs are found in both MyHours and SchoolHours. You can access these reports in summarized or free date range spans. You can also print out your reports.
